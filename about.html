Our Story

25 years of celebrating Indian textile heritage through art, craftsmanship, and culture.

Est. 2000

Woven in Tradition, Crafted with Love

Manas was born from a simple belief — that every thread of Indian fabric carries centuries of culture, art, and identity. Founded in the bustling bazaars of Chandni Chowk in 2000 by Manas Gupta, the brand began as a humble workshop dedicated to preserving the dying art of hand embroidery.

Over 25 years, we have grown into a beloved brand spanning across India, yet every piece we create still holds the warmth of that original workshop. We partner with over 200 artisans across Varanasi, Jaipur, Lucknow, and Kolkata — giving voice and livelihood to master craftsmen whose skills might otherwise be lost to time.

Today, Manas is more than just clothing. It is a commitment — to heritage, to quality, and to the belief that traditional Indian fashion can be modern, accessible, and breathtaking.
